Smart Part-Time Shift Allocation Platform

Smart Shift is a mobile workforce management and shift booking platform designed to streamline part-time shift allocation for employers and workers. It automates shift publishing, eligibility checking, booking, and performance tracking using a skills-based and points-driven system, reducing cancellations and improving fairness in workforce allocation.
